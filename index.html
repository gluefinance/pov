snapshot by gluefinance

Take a snapshot or rollback all your stored procedures in your PostgreSQL database.

snapshot can take a snapshot of all your database functions and objects depending on them, such as constraints and views using functions. snapshot can rollback to a previous snapshot without modifying any of your data or tables. It will only execute the minimum set of drop/create commands to carry out the rollback.

Dependencies

contrib/pgcrypto

Install

psql [database] -f install.sql
